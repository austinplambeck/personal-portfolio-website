"TOMORROW'S STORM"

A Fantasy Adventure
Coming Spring 2022
to Paperback and Kindle

"Everything changes for Polaris and Cyrus when a fallen star crashes nearby, opening a portal to a barren, hellish world — the opposite of their own. They soon discover dangerous magical powers, a long-forgotten prophecy foretelling their world's doom, and a young King determined to turn that prophecy into reality."

Other Works in Progress

Dark Fairytale Anthology

  • 5-8 short stories inspired by classic fairytales and myths, in dark & earthy settings
  • Some recurring themes: the transformative power of love, redemption, breaking toxic cycles, combatting & accepting darkness, and finding hope in tragedy.
  • Published intermittently, with the collection coming either late 2022 or early 2023

Mythic Adventure Game

  • Text-only, choice-based adventure game inspired by mythology and the philosophies of Joseph Campbell
  • "You and your tribe have been cast out of your ancient lands by the tyrannical empire. As your tribe seeks refuge in the desert, a prophecy comes to light, and it calls upon you. It tells of a hero who must travel across the continent to the Eastern Mountain Range, in search of the World Tree. The fabled spirit dwelling there is said to grant an ultimate power to those who can find it. A power that might be the key to defeating the empire…"
  • Coming Summer 2022

Other WIPs

  • “Apollo” — a Tomorrow’s Storm spin-off novella
  • “The Ascent of Vaq Vyla” — an epic sci-fi fantasy inspired by the myth of Orpheus and Eurydice
  • “Sun’s Blood” — the sequel to Tomorrow’s Storm
